NAME: Sun Mullnir

ANCESTRY: Goblin

BACKGROUND: Feral Child

CLASS: Barbarian (Druid Dedication)

SPECIALTY: 2-handed weapon wielding! Hammer go smash. Longaxe go chop chop. Also mundane healing.

A simple drummer and chef with a big heart. Very protective of her own. Somehow she ended up as the successor to the Mog-Ur, a spiritual leader of sorts for the settlement of Armings hidden within the Neverwinter Woods.
