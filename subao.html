NAME: Subao

ANCESTRY: (Shadow) Dragonborn

BACKGROUND: Returned

CLASS: Fighter (Rogue Dedication)

SPECIALTY: Melee Striking/Single Target Lockdown/Grapple/Intimidate

Subao is a sellsword who has come into contact with a sentient weapon in his travels with the group. After being slain by a foe, this weapon has returned him to life and the two are now bound together. He is currently in a relationship with Bell and the guild leader of a bounty hunting guild.
